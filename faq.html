General Info.

Grand Theft Auto is developed by Rockstar North, a subsidiary of Rockstar Games.

The first Grand Theft Auto game was released in 1997.

Grand Theft Auto engages players through immersive narratives, open-world exploration, and dynamic crime-centric gameplay.

About our games

Rockstar Games typically announces release details for new GTA titles or expansions closer to their launch dates.

Stay tuned to our official channels and news updates for the latest information on upcoming releases and additional content.

Grand Theft Auto games have a vibrant modding community, allowing players to customize their gaming experience. While Rockstar Games may not officially endorse all mods, some are supported, and the community's creativity is encouraged. Players should refer to the modding guidelines and community forums for safe and enjoyable customization options.
